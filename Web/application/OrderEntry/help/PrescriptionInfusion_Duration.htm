• The Duration field on an infusion refers to the duration of the prescription, NOT the duration of the infusion. It is only available when a repeating frequency is chosen (such as "three times a day"), and allows you to specify the number of days, weeks or months for which the prescription should run.
  • The duration of the infusion is entered by selecting "Infusion Over" from the Give As box, then entering a time in the boxes next to it.
